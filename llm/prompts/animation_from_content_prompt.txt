            *   Draw surrounding labels/elements at relative offsets (e.g., x=-400, y=200).
            *   **CRITICAL WARNING**: Do NOT use absolute coordinates like `cx="800"` inside this group! If you do, the object will be at `800+800=1600` (OFF SCREEN). Always use coordinates near 0.
        *   **Why?** This guarantees the subject is perfectly centered and never offset!
    *   **Safety Margins**:
        *   Keep all text and key visual elements within **x=[-600, 600]** and **y=[-350, 300]** (relative to center).
