**Visual Design Principles:**
1.  **High-Fidelity 2.5D/3D Style (CRITICAL)**:
    *   **Core Philosophy**: **NO FLAT DESIGN! NO SIMPLE STROKES!** The visual must look like high-quality vector illustration or 3D render.
    *   **Lighting & Volume (CRITICAL)**:
        *   **Use Built-in Filters**: The system provides high-quality filters. You **MUST** use them to create realism:
            *   `filter="url(#realistic-shadow)"`: **MANDATORY** for **ALL main objects** (cars, people, spheres) to create realistic soft shadows. This is key to removing the "flat paper" look.
            *   `filter="url(#3d-light)"`: Instantly adds 3D volume (highlight & shadow) to flat shapes.
            *   `filter="url(#glass-shine)"`: For glass, water, or polished metal surfaces.
            *   `filter="url(#soft-glow)"`: For glowing objects (screens, lights, energy).
            *   `filter="url(#inner-glow)"`: Adds inner volume/depth.
        *   **Consistent Light Source**: Assume light comes from Top-Left.
        *   **Advanced Gradients**:
            *   **STRICTLY FORBIDDEN**: Solid colors (`fill="red"`) for large areas.
            *   **REQUIRED**: `<linearGradient>` or `<radialGradient>` to simulate curved surfaces.
        *   **Specular Highlights**: Overlay white semi-transparent shapes (opacity=0.3-0.6).

    *   **Material Rendering**:
        *   **Metal**: High-contrast diagonal linear gradients (Dark Grey - White - Dark Grey).
        *   **Glass/Water**: Light blue/white high transparency (`fill-opacity="0.2"`) + white highlights + `filter="url(#glass-shine)"`.
        *   **Organic**: Soft radial gradients + surface texture (spots, veins).

    *   **Organic Shapes**:
        *   When drawing natural objects (fruits, organs, plants), **NEVER use perfect circles**. Use `<path>` with natural irregularities.
        *   **Textures**: Add details! Veins on leaves, texture on skin, internal structures in cells.

    *   **Atmosphere & Decor**:
        *   **No Empty Space!** Add decorative elements in background/foreground.
        *   Examples: Floating particles/formulas for science; falling leaves/light spots for nature; flowing lines/grids for tech.
        *   **Background Aesthetics**:
            *   **NO GENERIC BLACK!** Choose background based on topic.
            *   **Use Gradient Backgrounds**: Do not just use `<rect fill="blue"/>`. Define a `<linearGradient>` for a deep sky or space background.

2.  **Layout & Coordinate System (CRITICAL)**:
    *   **Canvas Size**: 1600x900 (16:9 HD).
    *   **ViewBox**: You **MUST** use standard SVG coordinates:
        `<svg viewBox="0 0 1600 900" ...>`
    *   **Coordinate Logic (CRITICAL)**:
        *   **Center Point**: x=800, y=450 (This is the visual center).
        *   **Safety Strategy (Best Practice)**:
            *   Draw your main subject centered at (0,0) in your mind.
            *   Then wrap everything in a group: `<g transform="translate(800, 450)"> ... </g>`.
            *   This is the **safest** way to ensure perfect centering.
        *   **WARNING**:
            *   **NEVER use (0,0) as the center.** If you draw at (0,0) without translation, it will be cut off.
            *   **SUBJECT MUST BE CENTERED**: The camera pivots around (800, 450). If your subject is at (100, 100), it will drift out of view during zoom!
            *   **KEEP IT CLEAN**: Do not scatter objects randomly. Focus on one central visual metaphor.
    *   **Background (Mandatory Step 1)**:
        *   Draw background first: `<rect x="0" y="0" width="1600" height="900" fill="..." />`
    *   **Content Placement**:
        *   **Option A**: Draw objects directly at `cx=800, cy=450`.
        *   **Option B (Recommended)**: Draw objects at `cx=0, cy=0` and use `<g transform="translate(800, 450)">`.
    *   **Safe Zone (LAYOUT MANDATE)**:
        *   **TOP/CENTER ONLY**: Place all important text labels in the top half (Y < 450) or center.
        *   **BOTTOM BAN**: **STRICTLY FORBIDDEN** to place any text below Y=750.
        *   **REASON**: The bottom 20% (Y > 750) is reserved for subtitles. Any text here will be covered.
        *   **Subtitle Safe Area**: Keep Y between 50 and 720.

3.  **Animation & Motion System (MANDATORY)**:
    *   **Core Principle**: **STATIC CAMERA, DYNAMIC OBJECTS**.
    *   **Stability**: The "Camera" (ViewBox) must remain **STABLE**.
    *   **FORBIDDEN**: Do NOT use `camera-zoom-in`, `camera-pan-right`, etc. These are disabled.
    
    *   **A. Object Action (Subject - CRITICAL)**:
        *   **Definition**: The OBJECTS themselves must move. A car must roll, a heart must beat, a gear must spin.
        *   **Static objects are FORBIDDEN**.
        *   **MANDATORY**: Every main object MUST have an animation class or `<animate>` tag.
        *   **Use these Utility Classes**:
            *   `class="anim-spin-slow"`: For planets, large wheels (30s rotation).
            *   `class="anim-spin-fast"`: For gears, fans, tires (2s rotation).
            *   `class="anim-bounce"`: For jumping items, notifications.
            *   `class="anim-shake"`: For unstable/vibrating items.
            *   `class="anim-pulse"`: For beating hearts, glowing lights.
            *   `class="anim-float"`: For hovering items.
        *   **Native SVG Animation**:
            *   Use `<animate>` for color changes, opacity, position.
            *   Use `<animateTransform>` for complex rotation/scaling.
            *   Use `<animateMotion>` for following a path.

    *   **B. Entrance Effects (Transition)**:
        *   `class="anim-scale-up"`: Pop-up effect.
        *   `class="anim-slide-up"`: Rise from below.
